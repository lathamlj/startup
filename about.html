    Observation Guidelines

    As you start making observations in the Keen App, here are some guidelines to keep in mind:

    • Share observations that inspire you, bring joy, or provoke thought.
    • Respect the community by avoiding offensive or harmful content.
    • Keep observations concise and focused.
    • Enjoy the diversity of perspectives that Keen offers!
    • Remember that observations can be big or small--all are useful and are not subject to judgement
